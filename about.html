About

A bit about me, what I do, and what drives me.

Hi, I'm Bradley

I'm a Business Analyst who thrives at the intersection of data and decision-making. In my day-to-day work, I use SQL and Power BI to build dashboards and reports that turn raw data into clear, actionable insights. There's something deeply satisfying about finding the story hidden inside a dataset.

Outside of work, I'm a self-taught developer. I build web applications and mobile apps — not because I have to, but because I genuinely enjoy the process of creating something from nothing. It keeps my problem-solving sharp and gives me a different lens for approaching challenges at work.

I believe the best work happens when analytical thinking meets creative execution. Whether it's a data model or a user interface, I care about getting the details right.
